# Meta Ad Performance & Marketing Analytics | Power BI


Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing digital marketing performance across Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram). It provides a deep dive into campaign efficiency, audience engagement, and ROI. The dashboard is designed to help marketing managers and stakeholders optimize their ad spend by identifying the highest-performing creatives and placements.

Purpose
The dashboard transforms complex transactional ad data into actionable insights by answering:
What is our Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) across different campaigns?
Which platforms (Facebook vs. Instagram) yield a lower Cost Per Click (CPC)?
How does audience demographic data correlate with conversion rates?
What are the trends in Reach and Impressions over the last quarter?

Tech Stack
Power BI Desktop: For data modeling and interactive reporting.
DAX: Used to create complex measures for CTR (Click-Through Rate), CPC, CPM, and ROAS.
Power Query (M): Used for data cleaning, merging disparate marketing reports, and handling date-time transformations.
Data Modeling: Implemented a Star/Snowflake Schema to connect Fact tables (Ad Events) with Dimension tables (Campaigns, Platforms, and Dates).

Key Insights & Features
Executive Summary: High-level KPIs including Total Spend, Total Conversions, and average Cost Per Result.
Campaign Drill-Down: Detailed view of individual campaign performance, allowing for a side-by-side comparison of budget vs. actual results.
Time Intelligence: MoM (Month-over-Month) and WoW (Week-over-Week) analysis to spot seasonal trends in ad performance.
Platform & Placement Analysis: Visual breakdown of where ads are served (Stories, Feed, Reels) and which placement delivers the best engagement.
Interactive Filtering: Users can filter by Date Range, Campaign Objective (Lead Gen, Awareness, Traffic), and Region.

How to Use
Use the Date Slicer at the top to select your reporting period.
Click on a specific Campaign Category to filter the visual charts for that objective.
Hover over the Trend Lines to see detailed tooltips on daily performance fluctuations.
